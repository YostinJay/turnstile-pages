REPRESENTATIONS, WARRANTIES AND UNDERTAKINGS

The Applicant represents, warrants and undertakes to RQAM that:

(a) the matters set forth in this form, including the reasons and purposes for the Applicant’s request for turnstile records and their proposed use are true and accurate;

(b) the turnstile records requested relate to employees or former employees of the Applicant;

(c) Applicant shall keep all turnstile records conﬁdential, and not make further disclosures of the turnstile records unless the Applicant shall have obtained the prior written consent of RQAM, and it shall only use and disclose the turnstile records disclosed to it by RQAM strictly for the purposes identiﬁed by the Applicant above ("Purpose"), and that it will ensure and procure that the turnstile records are only accessed by the Applicant’s employees strictly on a need-to-know basis;

(d) it will only use the turnstile records within Singapore and not transfer the same overseas;

(e) it has at all times complied with and will continue to comply with the requirements of the PDPA in respect of the collection, use, disclosure and other handling of any personal data comprised within the turnstile records. The Applicant shall provide RQAM such assistance as it may require in meeting RQAM’s own obligations under the PDPA;

(f) without prejudice to the generality of the foregoing, it shall employ reasonable administrative, physical and technical safeguards to ensure that the turnstile records are afforded protection in accordance with the PDPA and to prevent unauthorised access, copying, modiﬁcation, storage, reproduction, display or distribution of the turnstile records;

(g) it shall not use any personal data comprised in the turnstile records for any other purpose other than the Purpose or to disclose such personal data in individually identiﬁable form without the express authorisation of RQAM; and

it shall immediately destroy the turnstile records when it is no longer required for the Purpose and notify RQAM thereof in writing.
